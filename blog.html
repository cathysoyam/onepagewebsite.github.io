A Captive
November 1, 2016

The land in between pages.

It’s the land I can explore freely. It’s the land that lets me feel free. It’s the land with no care of my opinions. It’s the land that makes me feel different emotions. It’s the land that makes me laugh, cry and angry.

It’s the land that gave me knowledge, wisdom and experience. It’s the land where I met lots of people. People with different personalities, attitudes and nationalities. It’s the land where I met Harry Potter, Professor Langdon, Princess Sultana, Edward Cullen, Thaddeus Arzadon and many more people.

It’s the land that reared my skills in writing. It’s the land that became my refuge when sad times comes and I needed my personal clown. It’s the land that served as my inspiration and motivation.

It’s the land that held me captive. Captive for years but it didn’t matter. Those years are my most memorable times … for I became a happy captive. A captive of the land in between the pages.
