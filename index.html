Poems

Inside

Covered in your shell

Veiled by your smile

Sheltered inside

The one who's colored black

Red and white

Love and purity

Yellow and purple

Happiness and loyalty

All of which

Covered it well

Veiled and sheltered

The glowing color inside

It glowed then faded

Faded then glowed

But still was sheltered

The one who's colored black

You hid it well

And surfaced once

Were you happy?

Were you sad?

Being You

As we keep our silence

We're being judged

As we find our voice

We're being compared

As we learn to love

We're being hated

As we start to hate

We're being loved

As complicated as it may be

As unpredictable it may seem

We're not to choose

'Cause this is our world

It's a world of no perfection

It's a world of stares and gossips

It's a world of similariries and differences

It's a world of love and hatred

But however, however

You and I should remember

Everyone is unique

Everyone is special

You are who you are

I am who I am

We're two different individuala

And we'll never ever be the same
